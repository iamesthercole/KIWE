About Me

I'm a soul/R&B singer/songwriter, who began ambitiously singing at my church in 2012. Since then I have gone on to write and record my own songs and have gone further in releasing my own fresh original music. I have also written songs for a movie God's Will and a brand song for a youth magazine called ‘You Are Beautiful’. I'm available for interviews and sessions to talk about my new music, artist development camps, events and podcasts. Please check out my music projects and contact me for my services.
